## Asuras

- The plan was for Kyralia to become the evil eventually defeated by the Asura Champion. For this purpose, they tipped off Kyralia to the existence of Kamino, enabling her to conquer worlds with overwhelming force. However, when the godhead proved incorruptible, the Asura pivoted to the Sith, encouraging the formation of kingdoms in the Outer Rim from the scattered Sith tribes.
- (It came to me in a dream) Movi being treated by breaking every cell in their body and making them relive that pain a thousand times over? Nymphadora of Tython involved with the Asura? The Tython Sith being an industry plant of the Asura?
- The Imperators were trained by the Asuras specifically to kill Movi
- In the training of the Movi, the Asuras suppressed the force-sensitivity of the subjects by killing the midichlorians in their bloodstream, but the force-sensitive subjects turned out to be the most promising candidates nonetheless. Later, a cosmic event caused a rapid rebound in the midichlorian population, which granted these Movi unparalleled affinity with the Force.
- The Asuras are a tribe possessing superior technology to the rest of the galaxy, such as bioweapons, advanced power sources, and higher grade weaponry, often made to counter force users. They also played a role in suppressing the advance of technology in the rest of the galaxy, to maintain their superiority.
- The Asura bioweapons propagate throughout a certain subspecies rapidly. There were 5 instances of a species being wiped out, corresponding to the 5 idols, hinting at the source of the power behind the weapon.
- The Asura Empire was highly hierarchical and class-based. The Asura order believed that kings were made not through circumstance, but through blood. As such, they coveted the young scions of the great houses of the galaxy, and sought to make them their highest-ranking agents.
- As a last resort, the Mahasurastra would be fired at full power, killing everyone in the galaxy save for the Movi, leaving them to rebuild the human species from a supposedly superior fresh start.
