> [!abstract]
> The KE (Kyralian Era) calendar was used by Kyralian scholars to date the years following Kyralia's ascension as god-emperor. 
> The ABK (After the Battle of Kyralia) calendar is used in Kyralia, to mark the ascension of Kalki (2023 CE). ABK = CE - 2023.
> The ASC (After the Scourge of Coruscant) calendar is used by the Sith, to mark the ascension of Orthus as the destroyer of evil (2028 CE).

## The Age of Reason (3923 BBK **-** 2316 BBK)
* `1900 BCE/3923 BBK` Meluhan immigrants arrive on the island's western shores
* `1800 BCE/3823 BBK` New Meluha is established
* `1550 BCE/3573 BBK` Meluhans develop spacefaring technology, expand rapidly
* `1545 BCE/3568 BBK` Battle of Yavin
- `1400 BCE/3423 BBK` The Jedi Order, and consequently the New Republic, collapses under Asura infiltration. The following century sees the resurgence of a Sith culture, which is quickly suppressed and marginalized.
* `502 BCE/2525 BBK` Dwarkan immigrants arrive on Meluha's western shores, as Kali takes root at the antipode
* `302 BCE/2325 BBK` Meluhan Civil War begins
* `293 BCE/2316 BBK` Meluhan Civil War ends
