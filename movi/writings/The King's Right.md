The Defender's Right was a tradition that spread throughout the Outer Rim in the wake of Lady Kyralia's ascension. This system was championed to maintain order and prevent total destruction of cultures in the event of Galactic wars.
Under King's Right, the sovereign of a territory could challenge that of the invader to a duel, which could not be refused under the divine laws of Kyralia. The Conqueror could either fight the challenger themself, or offer a champion to do the same.
