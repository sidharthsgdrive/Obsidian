For eons we have languished under the weight of our past; of civilizations long buried and stories long forgotten. We have tried in vain to restore some semblance of the former glory of the Stars, by warring for dominance and control. But is that why we exist, in this vast expanse?

I think not. We stand at the precipice of a new age. An age lived by beings, not in service to some greater cause far divorced from their lives, but in effort to seek out their own existence and better that of those around them. One ruled not by lords who seek to expand, conquer and enslave, but by those who look inwards, to prosper and care for their people. One presided over, not by gods who rule, but by gods who guide and comfort -- Gods who live and die as men!

Revert! 
We are not ready for a unified Galaxy, not yet. The histories tell of countless ages when individuals thought they were destined to rule the stars, and crushed those stars in the attempt to claim them. 
Revert! 
The Asura took it upon themselves to bring an Age we were not ready for. But we cannot fault their restraint in damming the flow of knowledge that could destroy us, nor their pragmatism in balancing the Great Powers against each other, nor their ambition, however flawed, in creating the Movi project. For it is these very actions which allow us to go back.
Revert! 

Yet I promise our descendants this -- There will come an Age of Reason yet, when the forces of Man, Machine, and Empire can work together without one part dominating the others.
