
Byss was once home to twin moons, each battlestations commissioned in a bygone age, before the Sith Renaissance. After the Kyralian rebellion, Lord Cronus ordered them destroyed and stationed a detachment of the Golden Fleet to guard against any future uprisings.

To the surprise of Teta, even the shards of the destroyed moons remained a formidable defense. The Presidency was faced with devastation when they attempted to storm the inner core - After weeks of braving the treacherous Byss Run, the Megalon fleet was torn to shreds upon exiting hyperspace.

The Shards of Byss are still seen from the surface of the planet, strikingly beautiful in their appearance, but a constant reminder to Exodus of his failure.
