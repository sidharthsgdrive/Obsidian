At the climax of Kervell's rebellion (`-17 BBK`) , Kyralia and Byss fought for dominion over the planet of Coruscant, which had passed between them for hundreds of years since the age of gods and was at the time under loose Kyralian authority, mediated by the Presidency which ran the planet for all practical purposes. 
In a decisive space battle -- the largest of the war -- the Kyralian forces were smashed by the Golden Fleet. Amidst the chaos, the ruling House of the Sun and Moon was destroyed in all but name, survived only by the captive Kervell. In an enigmatic gesture that has puzzled the galaxy ever since, the Dark Council chose to spare the man who started the uprising in the first place and withdrew the Fleet into the Inner Core. This was especially baffling, given the ruthless and unforgiving nature of the Dark Lord Cronus, who most expected to march on the Outer Rim and end the Kyralian civilization once and for all.
