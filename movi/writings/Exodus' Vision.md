Exodus grew up listening to stories of Darth Cronus' valor as head of the Golden Fleet. In these embellished tales of the subjugation of whole planets, he glimpsed the cruelty beneath the veneer of glory, and the larger meaninglessness in Man's wish to conquer the stars. 
*****
After his father's death, shortly before he was sent to Tython for training, Exodus dreamed of a great deluge that engulfed the galaxy in darkness, bringing about a great divide and restoring civilizations to their pre-galactic innocence. Kervell told him of the Kyralian myths of cyclic creation, propagation and destruction, deepening his belief in the need of a great reset.
*****
Despite his friend's counsel, the now Emperor of Kyralia did not waver in his territorial aggressions. Exodus witnessed firsthand the devastation of Tython and Byss by the Golden Fleet that followed the rebellion. Crushed by the loss of his family, he vowed to bring an end to the star wars.
