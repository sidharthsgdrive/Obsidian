* [Quiet moments](quiet_moments.md)
* [Chores](chores.md)
* [Rituals](rituals.md)

The activities near the top of the spectrum require more mental bandwidth and focus, while the ones near the bottom are near effortless, or otherwise require less [resistance](resistance.md) to get started, continue and end.
