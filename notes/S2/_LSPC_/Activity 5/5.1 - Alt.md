Problem statement: Car Accidents due to Human Error
Car accidents are a leading cause of injury and death worldwide, with human error accounting for over 90% of crashes. Despite advancements in vehicle safety, traditional driving methods remain prone to human limitations. Addressing this issue requires technological interventions, including Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS), autonomous vehicles, and AI-driven traffic management, to minimize human error and enhance road safety.
