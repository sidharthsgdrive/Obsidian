As part of the LSPC test, we were required to take an online test to assess our listening skills and ability to comprehend spoken English. I ended up scoring 38/40 on the test - I had to rewind a couple of words that I had missed, so I didn't count those towards my score. The test topics included sales conversations, scientific articles and a description of a city's landmarks. One of the challenges I faced was the heavy British accent of some of the speakers, which threw me off a bit. Overall, it was a fun challenge, and I could certainly improve my listening skills by paying a little more attention to the speaker.
