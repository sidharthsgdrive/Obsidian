Skill Development Activities During B.Tech
    •    Develop curiosity and understand the fundamentals of every topic you learn, enabling you to apply knowledge across different contexts and scenarios. Ask critical questions such as:
    ◦    Why am I learning this topic?
    ◦    Where is it used in real life?
    ◦    Why does it work?

Recommended Activities During B.Tech
    •    Engage in non-technical activities (e.g., art, sports, volunteering) that encourage interaction with students from other engineering disciplines.
    •    Participate in study tours and group trips with classmates.
    •    Take part in hackathons and design competitions to enhance problem-solving and collaboration skills.
