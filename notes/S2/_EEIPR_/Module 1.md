#### **2.1 Types of IPR**  
1. **Patents**:  
   - Scope (medical tech, biotech, engineering, etc.).  
   - Rights granted (exclusivity, commercialization).  
2. **Copyrights**:  
   - Protection for original works (books, music, software).  
3. **Trademarks**:  
   - Definition, examples (Pepsi®, Coca-Cola bottle design).  
   - Functions (source identification, legal protection).  
4. **Trade Secrets**:  
   - Criteria (commercial value, secrecy).  
   - Examples (formulas, customer lists, marketing strategies).  

#### **2.2 Strategies for Protecting IP**  
- Methods: patents, trade secrets, confidentiality agreements, security measures, IP audits, IP strategy.  
- Additional strategies: registration, monitoring, enforcement, education.  

#### **2.3 Role of IPR in Securing Funding & Competitive Advantage**  
- Investor confidence, collateral for financing, competitive differentiation (design protection, licensing, market trends).

### **4.  Identifying Pain Points & Problem Statements**    
 **4.1 Idea Generation Techniques**
1. **Understanding the Problem**: Problem definition, information gathering, stakeholder identification.  
2. **Towards Solving the Problem**: Mind Mapping, SCAMPER, Lateral Thinking.  
3. **Reverse Brainstorming**: Define problem, reverse goal, evaluate solutions.  
4. **Group Creativity**: Diversity of perspectives, brainstorming, collaboration.  
5. **Focus Groups**: Small group discussions, structured interaction.  
6. **Synectics**: Analogies, metaphors, playful environment.  
7. **The Gordon Method**: Clear problem statement, organizational alignment, triggers.  
**4.2  Developing and Refining Ideas**  
- Strategies: brainstorming, feedback, refinement, testing, collaboration, research, prototyping.  
- **Validation Process**: Feedback & evaluation → Refine concept → Test assumptions → Validated concept.  
**4.3 Strategies for Bringing Innovation to Life**  
- Key steps: define objectives, collaborate, prototype, demonstrate customer benefit, build business case, allocate resources, find partners, consider timing.
