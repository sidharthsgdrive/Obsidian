# Problem and Solution canvas preparation (Questions come from individual sections, not as a whole)

1. **Market Segmentation**: 
	* *Demographic* (age, gender, income, education)
	* *Psychographic* (lifestyles, values, personalities)
	* *Behavioural* (purchasing behaviours, brand loyalty)
	* *Geographic* (location, climate, culture)
	Benefits include improved customer satisfaction, competitive differentiator, increased campaign performance
***
2. **Value Proposition**:
	* *Identify benefits* of product/service
	* *Communicate value* of benefits
	* *Identify problem* faced by consumer
	* *Relate problem* to value provided by product
	VP differs from USP (unique selling proposition), in that it focuses on the overall value that the product/service provides to customers.
***
3. **Competitive Analysis**:
	* *Select competitors* to perform analysis
	* Conduct *market research*
	* Compare *product features*
	* Compare *product marketing*
	* Use *SWOT analysis*
	* Identify our place in the market
***
4. **Market Entry Strategies**:
	* Exporting
	* Franchising
	* Licensing
	* Subsidiaries and Acquisitions
	The three primary factors affecting the choice of strategy are **marketing** (which segment to target), **sourcing** (how to product the product) and **control** (whether to work independently or partner with other businesses)
***
5. **Market Validation**:
	 Presenting our product to the market to see if it is worth pursuing and understand the target market's needs and dynamics (**product-market fit**)
	The **steps of market validation** are: 
	* Assess business goals
	* Create test questions to ask potential customers
	* Find participants to interview
	* Conduct surveys or interviews
	* Analyze the data
	* Proceed with A/B testing, alpha and beta testing
	The goal is to use methods like **surveys**, **interviews**, **focus groups**, **prototyping**, and **minimum viable product** to get feedback and iterate based on the feedback to better suit the product to the market.
***
