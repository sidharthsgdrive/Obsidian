### **Module-4: Prototype Development Stakeholder Engagement Strategies**  

**1) Investors**  
- **Types**: Angel Investors (early-stage equity), Venture Capitalists (growth-stage equity), P2P Lending, Personal/Institutional Investors.  
- **Strategies**: Long-term focus, equity/debt investments, differentiation between passive/active roles.  

 **2) Partners**  
- Key partners: Manufacturers, Design Firms, Product Development Companies, Machine Shops, Engineering Firms.  

 **3) Customers**  
- Roles: Define target users, provide feedback, participate in prototyping.  

 **4) Advisors & Mentors**  
- **Core Functions**:  
  - Oversee design (e.g., CAD for automotive parts).  
  - Material selection (e.g., 3D-printed drone frames).  
  - Testing protocols (e.g., mobile app usability).  
  - Compliance assurance (e.g., FDA standards for medical devices).  
- **Mentor Importance**: Accelerate learning, avoid pitfalls, build confidence, expand networks, refine iterations, balance innovation/feasibility.
