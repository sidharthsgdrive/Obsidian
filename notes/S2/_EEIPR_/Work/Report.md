

**1. Executive Summary**  
Hexent is a novel multi‑scented perfume system that allows users to carry, switch, and blend up to four distinct fragrances in a single, refillable bottle. Leveraging patented multi‑chamber dispensing technology and AI‑driven scent recommendations, Hexent addresses the inconvenience, cost, and storage issues of traditional perfume ownership. Targeting fragrance enthusiasts, young professionals, luxury‑conscious consumers, frequent travelers, and eco‑minded individuals, Hexent will launch with a three‑tiered product line—Standard, Premium, and Luxury—priced between ₹1,500 and ₹3,000+. Revenue streams include direct retail, e‑commerce, subscription refills, licensing of the dispensing tech, and bespoke corporate gifting services. Initial funding will support R&D, tooling for modular bottle manufacturing, influencer‑led marketing campaigns, and pilot production of 10,000 units, aiming for break‑even by Year 2 and 30% gross margins thereafter.
