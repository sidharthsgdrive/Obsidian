1 - **Customer Segment(s)**
* Fragrance enthusiasts & collectors: 
	* These are people who are already invested in the consumer perfume market.
	* Their prior interest in designer perfumes makes them ideal customers for this unique product.
* Young professionals & Urban Dwellers:
	* The youth are another important segment to consider for this product.
	* This segment might appreciate a versatile, space-saving solution for carrying different perfumes for different occasions.
* Luxury & Fashion-Conscious consumers: 
	* The product would be an innovative and stylish addition to the high-end market.
	* This segment offers customers who follow fashion trends and want convenience without compromising on elegance.
3 - Triggers to Act
* Trend influence: 
	* Exposure to influencer reviews or social media trends could alert the consumer to the product.
	* The resulting enthusiasm around layering and personalizing scents would trigger them to try it out for themselves.
* Special Occasions & Gifting: 
	* This is a common trigger applicable to most products, but especially important for this product, because of its nature as an ideal gift item.
	* Holidays such as Christmas, Valentine's Day, etc would be ideal occasions to capitalise on the customer's need for fashionable and innovative gifts.
* Convenience & Lifestyle changes:
	* The desire for a convenient, all-in-one fragrance solution could be another powerful trigger.
4 - Emotions (Before/After)
* Before purchasing:
	* **Frustration** and **inconvenience** of having to struggle with multiple perfume bottles and limited space.
	* **Boredom** of the stale and saturated perfume industry.
	* **Excitement and Curiosity** upon discovering the solutions offered by the product
* After purchasing:
	* **Satisfaction** from the newly found convenience and versatility.
	* **Pride and Exclusivity** are some powerful emotions that can also drive further sales.
	* **Brand loyalty**: If the customer particularly enjoys the product, they may become loyal customers, and recommend it to their social circle, advocating for the brand.
