## Technical explanation
Now we'll dive into an explanation of the tech stack we used to implement the project.
For the frontend, we used Next JS and Typescript, and Tailwind and Shadcn UI for design.
Meanwhile, our backend runs on Flask 3.1 with Python, integrating the Gemini 2.0 Flash model as the primary AI, with OpenAI and Anthropic fallbacks.
The core engines include AI topic filtering, a Smart Scheduler, Real-time Analytics, and an AI tutor for step-by-step solutions.
## Innovation and impact
One of our key innovations is a 4-phase learning progression system, and an AI architecture that enables robust personalization. Our real-time dashboard and assessments accelerate students' mastery over the subject, while reducing planning effort for teachers.
The main distinguishing feature of our platform is the dynamic allocation of study materials, time blocks, and quizzes. These are generated based on the specific syllabus of the subject provided, but also take into account the student's preferences for time and difficulty.
The projected impact includes up to a 40% improvement in student learning efficiency, and a 60% reduction in teacher planing time.
