### Introduction
Hello and welcome to our project showcase for Srishti 2025.  We are a team from the CSE department of GEC Thrissur and in this video, I'm going to be presenting our team's project, entitled "Xenia study planner" in the domain AI and IoT For education and medical institutions. Our team members are Gabriel, Nevin, Abhiram, and myself Sidharth.
The problem we are addressing with this project is the one-size-fits-all nature of conventional learning approaches. As we know, under the traditional system, the diverse needs of students are often not met, and teachers are burdened by the need of manual planning and assessment for each class.
Our idea is an AI-driven platform that, given a syllabus and study materials, generates a study plan, schedules and assessments based on the topics provided. With this, it detects the student's weak areas, and dynamically provides resources and plans with continuous updates.
With this, we aim to achieve personalized learning paths, reduced manual planning for educators, and valuable progress insights for students, teachers, and parents.
