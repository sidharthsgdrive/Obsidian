1. **Install[ google drive for desktop](https://support.google.com/drive/answer/10838124)** , _it would be better to create a fresh account to use with this_ 
2. **Select any laptop folder to backup to google drive** *(i don't remember the exact process, just fuck around in the gdrive windows app ), I wouldn't recommend selecting the folder of your current Obsidian vault, just do another one just incase the information is lost, and you could copy paste the files later.*
3. **Create a new vault on the Obsidian windows app, selecting the backed up folder** _(create vault using existing folder)_
4. **Get the [drivesync app](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.ttxapps.drivesync)** and after signing in with the google acc you used to set up drive on laptop, **select the gdrive folder that you are using for the vault and sync it to a new folder on your phone** *(again, I don't remember the exact process, you will see the steps when you install the app)*
5. _Now the contents of the folder on your laptop should be synced to the contents of the folder you created on your phone._ So all you have left to do is to **create a new vault on your phone's Obsidian app using that synced folder as location.**
***
# Notes
* You can change the sync timing in the drivesync app, I have it set to around 5 minutes, and if its a file that you need immediately, there is also a "sync now" button in the drivesync app.
* To get the contents of the old vault into this one, just copy all the contents from that folder and paste it into the backed up folder, on either phone or laptop.
