### Thoughts

On the matter of **to-do lists**:
The question of whether to note down tasks in increasing or decreasing order of difficulty is an age-old one, as far as I am concerned. Reflecting on my behavior with respect to the daily note todolist I've maintained for a little less than a month, I've noticed that putting a more daunting task (one with more resistance involved) at the top of the list results in my **ignoring the list altogether** in the process of minimizing resistance. To make things simple, I think I should sort lists from more fun to less fun -- it's better to get some tasks done than none, and this would at least help me climb the ladder a little.
