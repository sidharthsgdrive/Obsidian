`29/06/25` 
![[Pasted image 20250629142020.png]]
It's clear that I've been doing the most typing by far in June than any other month ever. The way I do it is I keep a YouTube video, usually ASOIAF lore or something like that (anything which can be comprehended entirely or almost entirely from audio and isn't too demanding of attention while still being somewhat interesting) open in the background while using monkeytype. This way, I find myself thinking about the contents of the typing test less, so the actual typing is more mindless and subconscious, while the parts of my brain that comprehend language are engaged by the YouTube video.
