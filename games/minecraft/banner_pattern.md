## steps
1. black banner + green dye => green cross
2. paper + bricks = field mason banner pattern
3. banner + black dye + field mason => field mason
4. paper + oxeye daisy = flower charge banner pattern
5. banner + grey dye + flower charge => flower charge
6. banner + green dye + flower charge => flower charge 
7. banner + black dye => inverted chevron

### materials
* green dye x2, grey dye x1, black dye x2
* banner x1
* paper x2, oxeye daisy x1, bricks x1
